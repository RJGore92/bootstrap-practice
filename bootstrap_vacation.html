Mystic Isles Resort

A magical resort where wonderful sights and otherworldly experiences await.

What we offer

  • Free transit between islands of various magical natures.
  • A bar that will serve drinks to lift your spirits, perhaps literally in some extent.
  • Training in beyond-this-world arts that will blow away and impress mainlanders and newcomers.
  • A world-class resort across four islands, each home to their own varieties of creatures and mystic ecosystems.

How to find us

There are several points in which you can access a plane to the Isles, though to do so, there are several transfer points on outlying islands. Mystics from the main islands guard these transfer points and carry rune keys to open the gateways to the "other side" where the magic domain of our islands hides. Once crossed over, there will be warded planes ready to transfer incoming and outgoing guests to the main islands where the resort is built upon, the largest island having its own runway for our transport planes to come in to deliver guests to and from our resort. Our main resort building is just a short ride northwest of the runway, easily identifiable by the mystic conduit spire that overlooks most of the runway and the ocean.
